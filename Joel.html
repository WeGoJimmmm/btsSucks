Continental GT 650

The Continental GT 650 is a classic motorcycle made by Royal Enfield.

Specifications

  • Engine: 648cc, air-cooled, SOHC, parallel-twin
  • Power: 47 horsepower
  • Torque: 52 Nm
  • Transmission: 6-speed
  • Brakes: Dual-channel ABS
  • Suspension: Telescopic front fork and twin gas-charged rear shock absorbers
  • Weight: 198 kg
